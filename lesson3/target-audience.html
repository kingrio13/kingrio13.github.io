Target Audience

All people who needs to get updated with the weather and forecast. As most of the time, our activity is depending on the current weather. How we dress and what we will bring depends on it either.

Persona 1:

Students sometimes bring what they only need in going to school. They might wonder about the weather today and what they will bring, will it be umbrella for sun or rain, or will it be a coat for snow.

That's why they will be needing world weather check! for latest update

Persona 2:

The Teacher's quorum president together with his counselor planned for activities. Will they push through their swimming activity or will they look for alternative as they're not sure with the weather this comming saturday.

That's why they will be needing world weather check! for seven day forecast

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Family activity, Will it sunny day or it will be snowy. will they pursuse the family outside? will it be in Mall or in Nature park.

Scenario 2: Food to prepare, Will it be a soup that good for rainy and winter season or will it be a food for sunny day with cold juice or a watermelon

Scenario 3: Dress for activity, will it be boots or sneakers or slippers.

Scenario 4: Should the goverment or school principal declare no classes because of heavy snow, or rain or will there be huricane warning

Scenario 5: For parents who has their children at school, will they fetch them as their will be typhoon and possible no available taxi or other public vehicle or will they just wait at home as the weather is fine.
