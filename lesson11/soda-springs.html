Our World BY Rio Michael

Weather seems to be stronger and stronger as time goes by. Many factor affecting it! one of the most we fear is the word "Global Warming". We can't deny that because of Global warming, weather is withering.

There's something we can do, aside from prepairing our 72 hour kit, or a month or year supply. We can do meaningful activities that helps and preserve nature. We can plant trees, beach cleaning and even simple things such as limiting the use of plastics.

Recycling is one of the best way, by small and simple things we do, great things will happen. We might not know we're already inspiring people to do thesame. They might not need someone to follow, let us be an example! Weather wont wither no more.
